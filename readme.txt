How to use + Changes:
  pridaný progress bar na sledovanie priebehu spracovania máp,
  THK mapy v majú osobitnú škálu, nie spoločnú ako to bolo predtým,
  je možné si nastaviť rozsah y-osy na spoločnom grafe profilu úberov. Defaultne nastavené na 3,5 um. Prípadne je k dispozicii možnosť Auto – pre „autoscale“.
Changes in v15: 
  Added Nanotopography tab - highlights surface topography by median filtering
  In main tab load .mat files to both before and after windows (can be the same files) and click "Start processing" even without any checkbox selected in the menu on the left side.
  Then go back to Nanotopography tba and click on the radio button (either Before or Atfer)
  List of the available wafer maps will populate - double click on a record and the map will appear in the right window.
